About Manual Entries

Manual entries let you override blocklist behavior for specific domains. Use allow to whitelist domains that are incorrectly blocked, or block to blacklist domains not covered by your blocklists.

After adding or removing entries, click Apply (generate RPZ) on the Blocklists page to regenerate the RPZ zones.
